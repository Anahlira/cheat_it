App Functionalities

  • Create test by pasting the questions from a document
  • Set correct answers for each question
  • Support for multiple question types (multiple choice, radio buttons, etc.)
  • Interactive test player with instant feedback
  • Track and display test results and performance
  • Share tests with others and explore a library of all created public tests

Our Story

Once upon a time, there were two students named Berna and Teodora. They were both passionate about learning and constantly seeking ways to improve their study habits.

However, they struggled to find a suitable app that allowed them to create custom tests and practice their knowledge in an interactive manner.

Frustrated with the available options, Berna and Teodora decided to take matters into their own hands. They embarked on a journey to build their own test app from scratch.

With countless hours of coding, designing, and brainstorming, they created an app that fulfilled their needs. It allowed them to create tests with personalized questions, set correct answers, and even play the tests in a fun and engaging way.

Now, Berna and Teodora use their app to study effectively and share their knowledge with others. They are proud of their creation and hope that it helps many students on their learning journey.

Join them on this adventure and discover the power of personalized test creation and interactive learning!
